[2 points] Show that the following lambda-terms are equivalent:
- S K K == I
- B == S (K S) K

[1] S K K == I

I = λx.x
S=λfgx.fx(gx)
K=λxy.x

S K K = (λfgx.fx(gx))(λxy.x)(λxy.x)
S K K = λx.Kx(Kx) -- f=K and g=K
S K K = λx.(λy.x)(Kx) -- apply K=Kx with K=λy.x
S K K = λx.x -- as (λy.x) takes any arg and returns x
S K K = I


[2] B == S (K S) K

B = λfgx.f(gx)
S = λfgx.fx(gx)
K = λxy.x

S (K S) K = (λfgx.fx(gx))(KS)K
S (K S) K = λx.(KS)x(Kx) -- f=KS and g=K
S (K S) K = λx.(λy.S)x(Kx) -- expand KS with K=λy.S
S (K S) K = λx.S(Kx)
S (K S) K = λx.(λfgz.fz(gz))Kx
S (K S) K = λfgx.f(gx)
S (K S) K = B
